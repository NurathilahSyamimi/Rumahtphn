Her Majesty's visit with her daughter, Tengku Puteri Jihan Azizah ‘Atiyatullah was accompanied by the Deputy Prime Minister, Datuk Seri Dr Wan Azizah Wan Ismail who is also the Minister of Family, Women and Community Development

At the ceremony, the Raja Permaisuri Agong shared her hand in cooking bubur lambuk using his special recipe as a breaking fast meal for the shelter

They visited the residence of the shelter which houses 117 underprivileged multi-racial children, aged under 12

Tunku Azizah also presented aid to 100 recipients from the welfare home donated by the Federal Territory Islamic Religious Council (MAIWP) and Ahmad Zaki Resources Berhad (AZRB). Rumah Kanak-Kanak Tengku Budriah houses underprivileged children from various backgrounds including neglected, abused, thrown away, involved in begging activities as well as fighting for custody

A total of 56 students and 8 instructors participated in the Gotong Royong Program

The group of volunteers who participated in the gotong royong cleaned up around the house by collecting and disposing of rubbish, cutting the grass and cutting down unnecessary trees. Others have cleaned the house with the task of sweeping and washing the floor, bathroom, kitchen and toilet. Apart from that, several volunteers have done repair work on the facilities and amenities of the house

Appreciation and Big Thanks

RKKTB expressed its appreciation and thanks to all parties involved for the success of the gotong royong. Among those who helped were the students and also the instructors of the Welding Technology, Refrigeration Technology and Air Conditioning and Electrical Technology workshops. The program which started at 8.00 am ended at 4.30 pm

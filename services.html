Our
Services
An overview of
what we do

We provide services that can make it easier
for residents and visitors to use any service
from us. Not only that, in terms of interior,
we provide service for the children so that
they are more comfortable and happy when
they are in this house.

1. Care and Protection

This orphanage will always look after the welfare of the children because after the death of a family member, they desperately need a place where they can always be cared for, protected and loved

2. Guidance and Counseling

Guidance counselling, also known as counselling and guidance, is the act of assisting people in discovering and realising their educational, professional, and psychological potentialities in order to maximise their degree of enjoyment and social contribution

3. Religious and Morals

The terms morality and religion signify two interrelated but separate concepts in the opinion of many people. Religion largely focuses on the interaction between people and a transcendent reality, whereas morality is supposed to relate to how human affairs are conducted and interpersonal relationships

4. Education

Education is the field that examines how to educate and learn in classrooms or surroundings that are similar to classrooms, as opposed to using different informal and informal socialisation approaches

5. Activities In and Outside

Such activities should be done in orphanages because with that, they can communicate, cooperate and get to know each other. If there is free time, the caretaker will take them to do outdoor activities such as exercising, playing badminton, doing activities according to the schedule and more

6. Medical Treatment and Health

Medical care refers to the management and attention given to the children in order to treat a condition. The children will always be given medical treatment in the event of injury, fever, illness and so on. with this, their welfare will always be taken care of and all their needs will always be provided for
